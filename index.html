    • About the Website Owner


    My name is Robin and I’m in the slow process of developing my green thumb. In the past, I have been known as somewhat of plant assassin, but not able to let a challenge go, I’m determined to have a house filled with luscious green plants. Every month I buy a new plant for me and my girlfriend Charlotte to take care of, but often the collection shrinks as quickly as it grows. I aim for our house to be the perfect lesbian stereotype, filled with cats and plants. I feel we are halfway there with 2 cats and 20 plants, but there's always room for more.




    The Webite's Aim


    This website provides an A-Z list of some of the house plants that I desire to own or already take care of. I will outline basic, easy to understand, care tips for: watering, sunlight preferences, required humidity, heat tolerance, the best potting soil to use and fertilization frequency. Hopefully the information I have collated will help somebody else to grow their green thumb alongside me, but in the meantime, this will be a great opportunity to develop some website building skills aswell as providing tips and tricks for avid plant collectors.
